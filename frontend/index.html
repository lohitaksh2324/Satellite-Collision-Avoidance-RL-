Glossary
💥 DANGER PAIR

A satellite & debris within the danger threshold. Dashed line = threat link. Ring = threat zone.

🔴 CRITICAL / 🟠 HIGH / WARN

CRITICAL <1 km · imminent
HIGH <2.5 km · act now

🔥 BURN / ΔV

Thruster firing changing velocity. ΔVx/y/z nudge per axis in m/s. Larger = more fuel.

⛽ FUEL

Cumulative |ΔV| burned. Real spacecraft have finite budget — minimize burns.
